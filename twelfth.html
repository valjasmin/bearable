Twelfth Grade Tips!

* Apply to a minimum of 2 scholarships per week because you dont want to get your acceptance letter and your financial aid packet has a gap in it.

* Take all of your classes in senior year serious because colleges still look to see if you are student that was puntual all four years.

* Start of strong you'll finish strong.

* Enjoy your school's festivities because you might not see any of your friends and peers after graduation.

* Do not stress about any college essays or scholarships. Take it easy.

* Prioritize your schedule by doing what's important and urgent first then the non-important and non-urgent last.

* Stay safe during senior year because a lot of people like to party and anything can happen.

* Make connections with all your teachers and staff members. Always build bridges not burn them
