Eleventh Grade Tips!

* This is the time where you need to focus on your classwork and do your homework to the best ability. Your GPA is harder to improve if its slacking.

* Make sure you take SAT and ACT some colleges/univerisities do not super score your exams.

* Start looking at the colleges you do want to attend and the why behind it. Make a list so once senior year is around you already have something completed.

* Stay involved in your extracurricular activities.

* Learn about financial aid.

* Begin to look at different scholarships and figure out the requirements.

* Apply for a part time or summer job

* Do college tours to schools you are interested in.

* Start working on your college application essays now so you'll be ahead of the game. I started my essay as soon as I got out of school now that schools just started in August i'm already finishing up my essay just proofreading it before I submit it in September.
