Ninth Grade Tips!

* dont buy too many supplies because you dont want the hassle of digging through your pencil pouch to find one thing in a sea full of erasers and glue sticks

* get enough sleep at night because you dont want to be tired in the morning.

* Colleges do look at your grades in highschool. If you start of strong you'll finish strong.

* Join extracuricular activities because it will look good on your resume and college application

* Don't be the obsessive newbie in the school. Trust me when incoming freshmen get to highschool they are so loud.

* Prioritize your schedule by doing what's important and urgent first then the non-important and non-urgent last.

* Think about taking dual enrollment courses at participating colleges. This will save you money for college courses and jumpstart on your career

* STUDY STUDY STUDY! I learned a trick to retain information when studying. You study a topic for 30 minutes straight then have a 15 minute break after. Doing this will help the brain remember information without having to remember and remember more information.

* Make connections with all your teachers and staff members. Always build bridges not burn them
