Tenth Grade Tips!

* Stay focused and do not get distracted.

* Start studying for SAT/ACT.

* Review your notes every night even if you don't have homework.

* Start your research on different types of colleges you might want to attend.

* Always talk to your High School Counselor.

* Prioritize your schedule by doing what's important and urgent first then the non-important and non-urgent last.
